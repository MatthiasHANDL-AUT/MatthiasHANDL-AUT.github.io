Racial Fairness

Every person deserves equal respect, safety, and opportunities.

✊

The Problem

Racism appears in schools, housing, media, and workplaces. It can limit opportunities and affect mental health. Fairness means listening, protecting rights, and removing barriers.

Examples: Unfair hiring decisions; students excluded or stereotyped because of their background.

What helps: Clear anti-discrimination policies, inclusive education, fair hiring processes, and reporting systems.
