Fairness to Planet Earth

Future generations deserve a healthy and stable planet.

🌍

The Problem

Climate change, pollution, and deforestation damage ecosystems and communities. People with fewer resources are often affected first and most strongly.

Examples: Flood risks increasing in vulnerable regions; plastic waste harming oceans and wildlife.

What helps: Use public transport, reduce waste, join cleanups, save energy, and support reforestation projects.
