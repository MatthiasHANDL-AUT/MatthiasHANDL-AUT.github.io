Fairness to Animals

Animals should be treated with care and dignity.

🐾

The Problem

Fair Animals means that animals are treated respectfully and in a species appropriate manner. This includes ensuring that they have enough space, healthy food and good living conditions and that they do not have to suffer unnecessarily. Products with the Fair Animal principle come from animal husbandry that is sustainable, responsible and transparent. Anyone who buys Fair Animal products supports animal protection, ethical farming and conscious consumption.

How can YOU help? Buy organic products for your health, the environment and fair conditions for animals and farmers.
