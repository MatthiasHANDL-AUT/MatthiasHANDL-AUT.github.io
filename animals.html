Fairness to Animals

Animals should be treated with care and dignity.

🐾

The Problem

Many animals suffer from neglect, illegal trade, or poor living conditions. Industrial farming and irresponsible pet ownership can lead to pain and stress for animals.

Examples: Overcrowded shelters; animals used in unsafe conditions for entertainment or production.

What helps: Adopt from shelters, support rescue groups, choose cruelty-free products, and educate about responsible care.
