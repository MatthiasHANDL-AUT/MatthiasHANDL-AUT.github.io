Fair Food

Food systems should be healthy, affordable, and accessible for everyone.

🥗

The Problem

In many places, healthy food is expensive while unhealthy food is cheap and easy to find. At the same time, supermarkets and households throw away large amounts of edible food.

Examples: Families skipping fresh food because of high prices; restaurants disposing of unsold meals every evening.

What helps: Community fridges, school meal programs, donation systems for leftovers, and better food planning at home.
