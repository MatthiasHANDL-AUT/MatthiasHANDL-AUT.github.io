Fair Food

Food systems should be healthy, affordable, and accessible for everyone.

🥗

BIO Food

In many places people are buying the cheap food instead oft the Bio food, the reasons are cheap and humans can save a lot of money. Organic farmers do not use synthetic chemical pesticides or artificial fertilizers, which reduces energy consumption and protects the soil. Conventional foods may contain residues of these substances. Organic animals have more space, more exercise and do not receive preventive antibiotics or growth hormones.

Examples: Families skipping fresh food because of high prices; restaurants disposing of unsold meals every evening.

What helps: Community fridges, school meal programs, donation systems for leftovers, and better food planning at home.
