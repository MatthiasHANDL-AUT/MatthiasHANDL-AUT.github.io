Fair Food

Fair Food means that food is produced under fair conditions for people, animals and the environment. This is about sustainable agriculture, fair pay for producers and the conscious use of resources. An important part of fair food is also reducing food waste and promoting regional and seasonal products.

How can YOU help? Don't waste food, buy more products that are organic or you can donate money for the workers on our website in just one click
